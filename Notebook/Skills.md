# What Are the Skills?

## Muscles
Tied To Actions
- Melee Damage
- Gathering Scale
- Bow Distance
- Projectile Distance

## Guts
Tied To Food & Damage Taken
- Max Health
- Metabolism
- Getting Up rate

## Tendons
Tied To Water & Actions Taken
- Sprint Speed
- Reload Speed
- Looting Speed
- Jump Height
- Time to Craft

## Spine
Overall Stat, Rewarded Uniquely, and for milestone marks.
**Rewarded For:**
	- Getting a kill
	- Swiping a keycard
	- getting shot by a unique player
	- 
- Increases Skillcap
